Musical Instrument


A musical instrument is a device created or adapted to make musical sounds. In principle, any object that produces sound can be considered a musical instrumentâ€”it is through purpose that the object becomes a musical instrument. A person who plays a musical instrument is known as an instrumentalist. The history of musical instruments dates to the beginnings of human culture. Early musical instruments may have been used for rituals, such as a horn to signal success on the hunt, or a drum in a religious ceremony. Cultures eventually developed composition and performance of melodies for entertainment. Musical instruments evolved in step with changing applications and technologies.

Different Musical Instruments


  • There are mainly four types of musical instruments:



  1. Stringed musical instuments
  2. Wind musical instuments
  3. Membrane musical instuments
  4. Plate Type musical instuments

String musical instuments

String musical istuments produses music by a streched string
Example- Guitar,Piano,Sitar


Wind musical instuments

Wind musical istuments produses music by a vibration of air columns inside them
Example- Flute,Shehnai


Membrane musical instuments

Membrane means a thin skin

Membrane musical istuments produses music by a vibration of thin streched membranes
Example- Tabla,Drums

Plate type musical instuments


Plate type musical istuments produses music by vibration of thick plates
Example- Cymbals(Manjira),Jal-tarang
